Robert Charest — Idea Showcase 2025

aka Glargod · A collection of this year’s most visionary and enduring concepts

AxialMag: The Jailbreaker Engine

A compact, fuel-free magnetic engine (7-piston design, ~100-150 lb) that outputs 2–10 kW—fitting in a Tesla frunk—and powers EVs with supercapacitors for near-infinite range and multi-generational energy legacy.

AI Remote Viewing Protocol

A bias-minimizing framework for AI-powered “remote viewing,” guiding perception through structured stages—perfect for blind or abstract targets.

Universal Coalescence Theory

A speculative cosmology positing “pleichyma,” a primordial fluid, as the unified root of time, motion, gravity, and consciousness.

TrustVote 3000

A next-gen voting system blending literacy requirements, digital ID, blockchain auditing, and paper backups to rebuild democratic trust.

COZAL System

A firearm retrofit that locks firing based on muzzle orientation, enhancing safety with sensor-driven precision.

Neuropolaris-ideogenes Hypothesis

Proposes a neural parasite as a driver of ideological rigidity, with diagnostic and treatment pathways explored.

Magneto-Thermovoltaic Device

A thermoelectric converter using magnets to enhance electrical output while cooling the heat source—prototype yields milliwatts, aiming for high efficiency.

Compost Pool Heater

Harvests microbial heat from compost (~0.5–1 kW) to slowly warm pool water—design is scalable and sustainable.

AVB²: All-Vehicle Black Box

A rugged, subscription-free telemetry recorder for any vehicle—logs GPS, IMU, crash data; works offline or via Wi-Fi/Bluetooth.

AIOS: Artificial Intelligence Operating System

An AI-native OS concept that auto-generates lean assembly apps from natural descriptions—no code, high performance, under 100 MB footprint.
